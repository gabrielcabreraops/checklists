Air Return Checklist

Gather Information / Air Return Reason.
Advise The AOC and allocate gate.
Advise SOC, Allocators and all pertinent departments. Example: MX/ Airfield OPS/ AGR.
Send Air return ORN e-mail.
Ask FD Crew if an emergency was declared and advise the SOC.
Advise AO to provide the option to deplane and notify SOC with the time it was done.
Obtain information about the issue from pertinent department and update the SOC with the information.
If the aircraft goes OOS, Collect FOB and send FOB e-mail.
After Departure Close ORN e-Mail.
